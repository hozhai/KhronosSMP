Applies to both the Minecraft and the Discord server.

## 1) Respect only: no insults, threats, harassment nor discrimination. Treat each other with respect.

Jokes are fine, but how fine they are aren't decided by you; they're decided by staff and by whoever you're talking to at the moment. If someone does not find the joke funny, it is no longer a joke.

## 2) No doxxing nor NSFW content allowed: sharing personal info or explicit material → kick.

Self explanatory; absolutely no NSFW content allowed either in the Discord server or in the Minecraft server. However, the staff will be lenient with NSFW **jokes** as we're all teenagers after all. Rule 1 stll applies.

## 3) Do not spam nor advertise without authorization.

If you'd like to collaborate with us, message a staff member instead.

## 4) Do not impersonate staff.

The only official staff roles are `Owner`, `Admin`, and `Dev`. In the Minecraft server, the official staff roles are only `Owner` and `Dev`.

## 5) Do not hack or use cheats: any use of illegal client or mod will result in a ban.

Anything that gives you an unfair advantage against others will be considered illegal and therefore be bannable. This could be anything from more _lowkey_ cheats such as Blink or X-Ray to straight up blatant cheats such as Kill-Aura or B-Hop.

## 6) Do not exploit bugs or dupes: report bugs; taking advantage leads to punishment.

Any exploitation of bugs or dupes, whether in the Vanilla game or our server's custom plugin, shall be reported to the staff. _You may even be rewarded_.

## 7) Do not grief or steal in the resource world: treat claims and items with respect — disputes are resolved through staff.

Griefing, stealing, and trapping **in** the **resource world** is an absolute no-go. If you are found to be griefing, stealing, or setting up intentional player traps, punishments will be issued.

Any disputes, as said above, are to be resolved through staff.

## 8) Respect the server's mechanics: do not try to manipulate systems such as time/temple/GUI for an unfair advantage.

Refer to rule 6.

## 9) Follow the staff's instructions: listen to our decisions; appeal through staff's DMs.

Please, we know (kind of) what we're doing. If you have an issue, let it pass through us; if you have a problem with another player, we'd love to hear that.

## 10) Follow Discord's TOS and Community Guidelines at all times.

You can find Discord's Terms of Service [here](https://discord.com/terms) and their Community Guidelines [here](https://discord.com/guidelines).
