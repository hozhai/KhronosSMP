_Applies to both the Minecraft and the Discord server._

## 1) Respect only: no insults, threats, harassment or discrimination. Treat each other with respect.

Jokes are fine, but how fine they are aren't decided by you; they're decided by staff and by whoever you're talking to at the moment. If someone does not find the joke funny, it is no longer a joke.

## 2) No doxxing or NSFW content allowed: sharing personal info or explicit material → kick.

Self-explanatory; absolutely no NSFW content allowed either in the Discord server or in the Minecraft server. The staff, however, will be lenient with slightly NSFW **jokes** as we're all human at the end of the day. Rule 1 still applies.

## 3) Do not spam or advertise without authorization.

If you'd like to collaborate with us, message a staff member instead.

## 4) Do not impersonate staff.

The only official staff roles are `Owner`, `Admin`, and `Dev`. In the Minecraft server, the official staff roles are only `Owner` and `Dev`. Staff members will always have official roles to identify them.

## 5) Do not hack or use cheats: any use of illegal client or mod will result in a ban.

Anything that gives you an unfair advantage against others will be considered illegal and therefore bannable. This could be anything from more subtle cheats such as Blink or X-Ray to blatant cheats such as Kill-Aura or B-Hop.

## 6) Do not exploit bugs or dupes: report bugs; taking advantage leads to punishment.

Any exploitation of bugs or dupes, whether in vanilla Minecraft or our server's custom plugin, shall be reported to the staff. _You may even be rewarded_.

## 7) Do not grief or steal in the resource world: treat claims and items with respect—disputes are resolved through staff.

Griefing, stealing, and trapping **in the resource world** is an absolute no-go. If you are found to be griefing, stealing, or setting up intentional player traps, punishments will be issued.

Any disputes are to be resolved through staff.

## 8) Respect the server's mechanics: do not try to manipulate systems such as time, the Temple, or the menu for an unfair advantage.

Exploiting mechanics to gain unfair advantages—such as duplicating items, manipulating the time system, or bypassing intended restrictions—is prohibited and treated the same as hacking.

## 9) Follow the staff's instructions: listen to our decisions; appeal through staff's DMs.

Please, we know what we're doing. If you have an issue, bring it to us; if you have a problem with another player, we'd love to hear about it so we can help resolve it.

## 10) Follow Discord's TOS and Community Guidelines at all times.

You can find Discord's Terms of Service [here](https://discord.com/terms) and their Community Guidelines [here](https://discord.com/guidelines).
