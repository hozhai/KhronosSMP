# You spawn in. Now what?

You look on the bottom half of your screen, above your hotbar. You have 1 hour on your timer. _What does this timer mean?_ Each player has a timer isolated from others and owned entirely by you; we'll talk about it later. You're at spawn, your timer is ticking, you now have two choices:

- Sprint a rather short distance towards the west, out of the spawn's safe area, through the PvP zone, and to the portal's safe area.

- Sprint a slightly larger distance towards the east, out of the spawn's safe area, through a slightly larger PvP zone, and to the Temple of Time's safe area.

By the way, the builds themselves are the safe areas. Check for a lime concrete line near a structure; stepping inside will allow you to be safe from PvP unless you were combat tagged previously. We'll talk about the Temple of TIme and the portal later; let's first discuss combat tagging and the PvP zone.

# The PvP Zone

![Illustrated Map of KhronosSMP](khronossmp_map_graphic.png)

\*The graph above is drawn over the actual world map of the spawn world, so it's relatively to scale.

Each safe area (portal, spawn, and temple) will be surrounded by a square with an outline consisting of 1 block wide lime concrete. Everything outside of that outline is the PvP zone.

In the PvP zone, you can:

- Attack others
- Be attacked by others
- Use wind charges
- Get combat tagged

In the PvP zone, or anywhere else in the spawn world, you cannot:

- Build blocks
- Destroy blocks
- Place boats or any kind of vehicles
- Use Ender Pearls
- Wear and/or use elytras

If you hit a player or get hit by a player in the PvP zone, you will get combat tagged. The combat tag lasts 30 seconds, and will refresh if the fight continues. Whilst in combat tag, you cannot enter safe areas, and you may not leave the game; if you do leave the game, you will instantly die and your items will be dropped on the ground.

## **Not Implemented:** There is a zone outside the regular PvP zone where you can do _all_ of the things above for PvP purposes, but we'll talk about that later.

Now that you know what lies ahead in your journey, let's see where you chose to go first.

# You choose to go to the Temple of Time first.

![KhronosSMP's Temple of Time](temple.png)

It's a giant... not exactly a dome-shaped structure, but rather a... giant sandstone mountain...?

You walk in, its hollowness only disturbed by the enormous floating nexus in the shape of a pyramid inside, along with an altar on the bottom and what seems to be fragments of the pyramid.

You walk on top of the altar, seems like there's a parkour course made out of the fragments of the pyramid. You start climbing...

...you reach the top, interact with the nexus' core, and receive **60 Fragments of Time**, which you internalize by right-clicking, adding 60 minutes to your timer. Good choice! Now you'll start with 2 hours instead of one.

The Temple of Time's nexus' core is your main source of time in KhronosSMP, having a cooldown of 1 hour and giving you enough time to sustain that hour.

## The Resource World

![A beautiful shot of the resource world](https://placehold.co/600x400)

Whether you gathered time beforehand or straight up ventured into the portal --- welcome to the resource world!

At first (when you have no spawnpoint set in the resource world), you'll spawn at **the Rift**, an invisible location that sits up above in the sky, bringing any players from the portal to the resource world. You will be given an _Ephemeral Elytra_, only temporary enough to let you get to a good place to start your adventure.

**VERY IMPORTANT**: If your inventory is full, and you have an equipment on your chestpiece slot, the elytra **will replace** your chestpiece and drop it on the ground below the Rift. The staff are **NOT** responsible for any items lost this way. _This unintended behavior may be fixed in the future_.

In the resource world, PvP is disabled; griefing, stealing, and trapping are also illegal (see [rules](/guide/rules)). You can build, destroy, interact, and kill animals. In fact --- you're encouraged to build your base here. Automatic farms are also allowed, as long as they don't start lagging the server.

# The Menu

![KhronosSMP's /menu command](https://placehold.co/600x200)

If you run `/m` or `/menu` in chat, a user interface will pop up allowing you to check out the following tabs: **Time Management**, **Teams**, **Travel**, **Skills**, and **Language**.

## Menu - Time Management

You can deinternalize time here. Just how you turn **Fragments of Time** into time in your counter by internalizing it through a right-click, you can deinternalize time --- going from your counter, to an item.

In case you got confused, the two types of time are the following:

- **Internalized time** - The time on your counter, ticking down each second.
- **Deinternalized/Uninternalized time** - The time you can hold as an item --- the item being the **Fragment of Time**.

After choosing the amount of minutes to deinternalize, the time will appear in the 3x3 grid. Be careful though --- as if you close the menu the deinternalized time will be dropped onto the ground if your inventory is full.

## Menu - Teams

While I could've told you this earlier, it is only now a good time to let you know about teams after you've settled and internalized some fresh time straight from the nexus' core. _You can always set up teams first before doing anything in the server_.

You can create a team yourself by opening the Teams tab and clicking on _Create Team_ while not currently being in a team. Type in the name of your future great team and start sending out invites!

If you've received a team invite, you'll receive a notification from which you will be able to join their team.

You can also always leave your current team in the teams tab back in `/m`.

## Menu - Travel

Wait --- the spawn world has a portal to get to the resource world, but the resource world has no portal to go back to the spawn world... how can this be?

Fear not, for there is the travel tab in the menu, which will instantly teleport you to portal safe area in the spawn world. You, however, cannot use the travel feature to teleport to the portal when you're already in the spawn world.
